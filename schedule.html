Good to Know

Schedule Notes

A few things to keep in mind before you book.

Adult Classes Are Sundays Only

All adult 18+ classes run on Sunday mornings. Three class options are available — Intro to Barre, Barre Flow, and Sculpt & Tone — back-to-back starting at 9 AM.

Competition Ages 6–18

Youth and teen competition classes run weekday afternoons and Saturday mornings. Solo lesson slots are available Mon, Wed, Fri evenings and all day Saturday.

Private Lessons by Appointment

Private and solo sessions are booked through Acuity based on Kyla's availability. Book as far in advance as possible — spots fill quickly around competition season.

Cancellations & Reschedules

Please cancel or reschedule at least 24 hours in advance through your Acuity confirmation email. Changes sync automatically to Kyla's iPhone calendar.
